Study Tips

Time Management

Create a study schedule and stick to it. Break down topics into manageable chunks.

Active Learning

Practice coding, solve problems, and explain concepts to others.

Group Study

Form study groups to discuss concepts and solve problems together.

Understanding

Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorizing.
