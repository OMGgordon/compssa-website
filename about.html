Our History

The journey of COMPSSA at the University of Ghana

COMPSSA was established in [Year] with the vision of creating a vibrant community for Computer Science students at the University of Ghana. What started as a small group of passionate students has grown into a dynamic organization that represents and serves the interests of all Computer Science students.
